Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish loved by people all over the world. It consists of a round, flat dough topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and meats. This recipe will guide you through making a delicious homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • Dried oregano and basil for sprinkling

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature setting (usually around 475°F or 245°C).
  6. Punch down the dough to remove air bubbles. Roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, and add your desired toppings.
  10. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil over the toppings.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  12. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
